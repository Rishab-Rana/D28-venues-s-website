About D28 Venues

D28 venues is a combination of different facilities at one place
We provide rooms and spaces for gatherings (20 to 150), event planning and event management.

Our Restaurant

Indulge your senses in the rich and flavorful world of Indian cuisine at our restaurant. Our chefs craft each dish with care, ensuring that every bite is a taste of authenticity. With a splendid terrace that overlooks majestic mountains, dining at D28 Venues is not just a meal but a journey into the heart of India.

Events & Celebrations

D28 Venues is more than just a restaurant. We take pride in making your special occasions truly extraordinary. Whether it's a birthday party, pre-wedding shoot, Mehandi ceremony, engagement, or any other important event, our party lounge and garden facilities provide the perfect backdrop. With impeccable event planning and management, we ensure that your celebrations are seamless and memorable.

Accommodations

Our comfortable and well-appointed rooms offer you a tranquil retreat with the added bonus of mountain views from the balcony. We have taken great care to make sure your stay with us is both pleasant and affordable. Quality and satisfaction are our top priorities.

The D28 Venues Experience

D28 Venues is not just a restaurant; it's a combination of different facilities all under one roof. Our commitment to providing an extraordinary experience extends to every aspect of your visit. With stunning surroundings, impeccable service, and a true taste of India, we are dedicated to making every moment memorable.

Come and experience the magic of D28 Venues, where nature, cuisine, and celebrations blend seamlessly to create an enchanting tapestry of Indian hospitality. We look forward to welcoming you to our world-class location where your every need is met with warmth and dedication.
